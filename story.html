Who We Are

Our Story

The Beginning

Born from a belief
that craft matters.

Velo Co. started with Marcus and a clear conviction: that haircare in Ann Arbor deserved something more intentional. Not just a service, but an experience — a space where technique meets genuine human connection.

In 2013, he opened the doors to what was then a single-chair studio on the corner of Liberty and Division. The name? Velo — from the Latin for "to unveil." Because Marcus believed that the best haircut does not change who you are. It reveals who you already are.

More than a decade later, that philosophy still lives in every shampoo, every snip, every conversation in our chairs.

Our Belief

Slow down.
Feel the difference.

In a world of fast appointments and conveyor-belt service, Velo Co. is a deliberate pause. A space where the music is right, the consultation is real, and the finish is always worth the wait.

How We Got Here

A decade of
growing with purpose.

2013

The First Chair

Marcus opens a single-chair studio on Liberty Street. Word spreads quickly — the waitlist forms before the paint is dry.

2015

The Team Begins

Nia joins as the second stylist. The studio expands to four chairs, and the first shampoo bowl is installed in-house.

2017

Color Program Launches

Elena joins as Color Director, bringing a refined editorial approach. The color program becomes the fastest-growing service at Velo Co.

2019

The Big Move

Velo Co. relocates to its current home — a full boutique studio with eight stations, a dedicated color bar, and a designed lounge.

2021

Through the Storm

Like all small businesses, we adapted. Curbside consultations, safety-first protocols, and a renewed appreciation for community kept us going.

2025

Still Here. Still Growing.

Seven stylists, 2,400+ clients, and the same singular focus. Velo Co. continues to be Ann Arbor's most trusted boutique studio.

What Guides Us

The values behind
every appointment.

01

Craft First

We invest in education, tools, and technique because the quality of our work is the only thing that truly speaks for itself.

02

Deep Listening

The consultation is not a formality. It is the most important part of the service. We ask questions, we listen, and we remember.

03

Intentional Space

Everything about our studio — the light, the music, the layout — is designed to help you feel calm, seen, and at ease.

04

Community Roots

Ann Arbor made us. We support local suppliers, hire locally, and stay deeply connected to the community we serve.

05

Sustainable Practices

From our color line choices to our waste management, we operate with a conscience — because the planet is everyone's concern.

06

Zero Ego

We are here for you, not for ourselves. No gatekeeping, no jargon, no judgment — just exceptional work done with genuine care.
