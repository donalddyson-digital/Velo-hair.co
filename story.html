Who We Are

Our Story

Born in Ann Arbor. Built on craft, community, and the belief that every person deserves to feel extraordinary.

The Beginning

Where it all started

Velo Co. Salon was founded in 2012 by Marcus Okafor, a Detroit-born stylist who relocated to Ann Arbor with a single vision: to build the salon he wished had existed for him growing up — one that celebrated every texture, every culture, every expression of self.

Starting with just two chairs and a waiting list, Marcus built Velo Co. from the ground up, bringing together a team that shared his belief that the best salons aren't just about hair — they're about connection. About truly listening before you ever pick up a pair of shears.

"I wanted Velo to feel like a conversation, not a transaction. You walk in, we talk, we listen — and then we create something that's entirely yours."

Today, Velo Co. is home to seven artists — stylists, colorists, and texture specialists — united by that same founding spirit. Twelve years later, the waiting list has grown. The mission hasn't changed.

What We Stand For

The Velo Values

These aren't just words on a wall. They're the principles that guide every cut, every color, and every conversation at Velo Co.

🎨

Artistry First

We approach every head of hair like a canvas. Technical mastery is the foundation; creativity is what transforms it into something unforgettable.

👂

Deep Listening

The best consultation starts before a single word is said. We pay attention to how you hold yourself, how you talk about your hair, what you don't say.

🌿

Inclusive Care

Every texture, every identity, every vision is welcome and celebrated here. We train continuously to serve all hair types with equal expertise and respect.

🔬

Continued Education

The beauty industry evolves constantly. Our team trains quarterly with leading educators to stay sharp, current, and ahead of every trend and technique.

🤝

Community Roots

We're proud to call Ann Arbor home. We give back through mentorship programs, local partnerships, and by supporting emerging talent in our community.

The Whole Experience

From the moment you walk in to the moment you leave, every detail matters. The lighting, the product, the conversation — it's all part of the service.

The Space

Designed to breathe

Our flagship location on South Main Street was designed with intention. Clean lines, natural light, and curated materials create an atmosphere that's calm without being cold, editorial without being intimidating.

We believe that where you get your hair done shapes how you feel about the experience. So we designed a space that feels like stepping into a magazine editorial — with the warmth of a neighborhood studio.

The interior features custom styling stations, a dedicated shampoo lounge, a waiting area that doesn't feel like waiting, and walls that change with the seasons, showcasing work from local artists.
