Our
Story

Velo Hair Co was born from a simple belief: that every person deserves a hair experience built around genuine artistry, deep listening, and uncompromising quality.

What began as Marcus's solo studio on a quiet Ann Arbor side street has grown into a seven-person collective of artists — each extraordinary in their own right, united by shared values and a relentless passion for craft.

"We don't just cut hair. We create the version of yourself you've always imagined."

— Marcus, Founder & Creative Director
The Beginning

Where it all started

Marcus opened Velo in 2012 after a decade of training under master stylists in Chicago, New York, and London. He returned to Ann Arbor — the city where he grew up — with a vision for something the community was missing: a salon that treated every client as a collaborator rather than a customer.

The name "Velo" comes from the concept of velocity and movement — hair in constant flow, style always evolving. It speaks to the kinetic energy of a great cut, the momentum of creative partnership, and the idea that style is never static.

In the first year, Velo operated as a two-chair studio. By word of mouth alone, the appointment books were full within six months. The reputation was built not on marketing, but on the quality of the work and the warmth of the experience.

The Space

A studio built with intention

The Velo studio was designed to feel like a private atelier — not a traditional salon floor. Every element, from the lighting temperature to the music curation, was chosen to create a calm, focused environment where both client and artist can do their best work.

We invested in the finest professional equipment, sourced sustainable and non-toxic products, and built a lounge where waiting feels like a pleasure rather than a necessity.

When you're in the chair at Velo, you're our only focus. Every artist is trained to be fully present — no rushing, no double-booking, no corners cut.

Artistry First

We approach every service as a creative act. No formula, no shortcuts — only thoughtful, skilled work that honors the individual in front of us.

Deep Listening

The best haircut starts before scissors are picked up. We listen — really listen — to understand your vision, your lifestyle, and your relationship with your hair.

Continuous Education

The beauty industry never stops evolving and neither do we. Our team trains regularly with global educators to stay at the forefront of technique and trend.

Inclusive Excellence

Velo serves every hair type, texture, gender, and identity with equal skill and respect. Our team is trained across all spectrums of hair care.

Community Roots

Ann Arbor made us. We support local, give back when we can, and believe a great neighborhood salon is a civic asset — not just a business.

Sustainability

We use and recommend products that are better for hair and for the planet. We've eliminated single-use plastics and partnered with sustainable brands.

The Future of Velo

Always growing,
always learning

As we grow, we remain committed to the principles that made us: small team, high standards, personal connection. Velo will never be a volume salon. We'd rather do fewer things beautifully.
