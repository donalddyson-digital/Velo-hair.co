Street parking is available on S. Main Street and adjacent side streets. Street parking is available on N. Main Street. The city parking structure on Troy Street is a 2-minute walk and offers affordable hourly rates. The studio is ADA accessible with a ramp entrance from the main sidewalk.

Before You Arrive

The Neighbourhood

Right in the
heart of Royal Oak.

We are located in the vibrant North Main corridor of Royal Oak, steps from the Downtown Arts District and within easy walking of the city's independent shopping strip. It is a neighbourhood full of independent restaurants, galleries, and boutiques — the kind of block worth taking a slow walk down after your appointment.

Nearby: The Royal Oak Farmers Market, Emagine Royal Oak, Main Street restaurants, and the Woodward Avenue arts corridor.
