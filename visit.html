Getting Here

By Car

Located in downtown Ann Arbor. Street parking available on S Main and Liberty. Structure parking at the Fourth & William garage (3 min walk).

By Bus

TheRide Route 4 stops one block away at Main & William. Route 16 stops at Main & Washington (2 min walk).

On Foot

A 10-minute walk from U of M Central Campus. Centrally located near Kerrytown, Zingerman's, and the Ann Arbor Farmers Market.

The Experience

What to expect at Velo

01

Arrive & Unwind

Settle into our lounge with a coffee, tea, or sparkling water. Browse looks, flip through magazines, or simply enjoy the calm before your appointment begins.

02

Your Consultation

Every service begins with a dedicated consultation. Your artist will listen to your goals, assess your hair, and collaborate with you on a plan — no guessing, no assumptions.

03

The Transformation

The work begins. Your artist is fully focused — no rushing, no double-booking. The service ends with styling guidance, product recommendations, and a finish you'll be proud to wear.
