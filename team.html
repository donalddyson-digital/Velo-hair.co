The People

Meet the Team

Seven Specialists. One Vision.

Craft is personal.
So are we.

Every stylist at Velo Co. was chosen not just for their technical skill, but for their ability to listen, connect, and make every client feel genuinely seen. We are a tight-knit team that pushes each other — and that shows in the work.

Marcus

Founder & Lead Stylist

With 12 years behind the chair and zero ego, Marcus built Velo Co. on the belief that a great haircut starts with a great conversation. His signature: architectural precision with a lived-in finish. Specializes in bespoke cuts, men's grooming, and the kind of consultation that makes you feel heard before a single snip.

Book with Marcus

Nia

Senior Stylist

Nia is Velo Co.'s textured hair specialist and a warm presence from the moment you sit down. She has a gift for reading curl patterns and creating shapes that celebrate natural texture. She also does exceptional lived-in color and blonding on a wide range of skin tones.

Book with Nia

Elena

Color Director

Elena leads our color program with an editorial sensibility and a technically obsessive approach to tone. Her balayage and freehand color work has a natural, dimensional quality that photographs beautifully and grows out even better. Often booked 6+ weeks in advance — plan accordingly.

Book with Elena

Kassa

Creative Director

With a background spanning editorial shoots, fashion weeks, and runway prep, Kassa brings an eye that is unmatched in its precision. He excels at bold transformations, structural cuts, and avant-garde styling for special occasions. If you want to take a risk that pays off, Kassa is your person.

Book with Kassa

Julian

Senior Barber

Julian is the kind of barber people drive across state lines for. His fades are clean, his tapers are flawless, and his beard work is a genuine art form. Equally at home doing a classic gentleman's cut or a contemporary textured style, Julian's chair is always one of the most requested in the studio.

Book with Julian

Chloe

Stylist & Bridal Lead

Sarah brings an infectious enthusiasm and a steady hand that has made her Velo Co.'s go-to for bridal and special occasion styling. Her updo and finishing work is precise and romantic in equal measure. She is also an excellent everyday stylist with a strong eye for shape and movement on medium-length hair.

Book with Chloe

David

Fade Specialist

David is Velo Co.'s youngest stylist and its most technically innovative. He came up in the competitive fade circuit, honing a level of clipper precision that still turns heads in the studio. He has expanded his range to include textured cuts and even some color work, but the fade is where he truly shines.

Book with David
