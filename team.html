The Artists

Seven hands. One house.

Every stylist at Velo is a specialist — a cutter, a colorist, a texture expert. Read their stories below, then request the artist whose craft is shaped around what you need.

Marcus

Founder · Master Cutter
Twelve years behind the chair

Marcus opened Velo in 2014 after a decade of cutting in Chicago, Toronto, and New York. He specializes in architectural shapes — bobs, lobs, and sharp shoulder-length work that moves. Annual education with Vidal Sassoon Academy.

BobsShape ChangeDry Cutting

Nia

Senior Stylist · Creative Director
Nine years behind the chair

Nia joined Velo in 2017 and now leads stylist development. Her cuts live at the intersection of precision and softness — sharp line work blended into the kind of texture you can wear on day four. Trained in London & Milan.

Lived-in CutsFringesEditorial

Sarah

Waves & Shape Specialist
Seven years behind the chair

Sarah is our wave whisperer — shag cuts, beachy layers, the kind of long hair that holds shape without needing heat every morning. She also runs our monthly education nights on bias cutting.

ShagsLong LayersWaves

David

Fade Specialist · Barber
Eight years behind the chair

David came up in traditional barbershops before joining Velo's grooming program in 2020. High, mid, low — his fades hold their line for weeks and grow out clean. Hot-towel certified, meticulous.

Skin FadesTapersLine-up

Elena

Master Colorist · Color Bar Lead
Eleven years behind the chair

Elena leads our color bar and mixes every formula by hand. Her balayage is known for softness at the root and brightness through the ends — color that grows out beautifully. Goldwell Master Colorist, 2023.

BalayageLived-in BlondeCorrection

Julian

Barber · Beard Architect
Six years behind the chair

Julian specializes in classic gentleman's cuts with a modern hand — scissor-over-comb work, beard sculpting, and traditional straight-razor finishes. He also hosts our hot-towel shave nights each winter.

Classic CutsBeard WorkStraight Razor

Kassa

Texture Specialist · Curl Coach
Ten years behind the chair

Kassa built our texture program from the ground up. Curl-by-curl cutting, silk presses, updos for natural hair — plus the education clients need to actually recreate their style at home. DevaCurl advanced certified.
