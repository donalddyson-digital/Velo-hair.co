The People Behind the Work

Meet Our Team

Every stylist at Velo Co. brings a unique perspective, trained eye, and genuine care for the people in their chair.

Founder & Lead Stylist

Est. 2012

Marcus

Founder · Creative Director · Color Specialist

Marcus opened Velo Co. with a clear vision: a salon that didn't ask clients to fit a mold. With over 15 years behind the chair — spanning New York, London, and Ann Arbor — he brings a global sensibility and deeply personal approach to every cut, color, and consultation.

He is nationally recognized for his work with textured hair and advanced color techniques, and his clients range from students to executives, each treated with the same unhurried attention.

"The best haircut you'll ever get is the one that makes you feel like yourself — only more so."
