Common Questions

FAQs

Do I need an appointment?

Yes — we are appointment-only. This ensures every client gets our full attention. Same-day appointments are sometimes available; call us to check.

What's your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee equal to 50% of the service cost.

Do you offer consultations?

Absolutely. First-time clients receive a complimentary 10-minute consultation before every service. Major transformations (color correction, etc.) may require a paid standalone consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash. Gratuity is always appreciated and can be added at checkout.

Is the salon accessible?

Yes. We have a ground-level entrance with no steps, an accessible restroom, and wide aisles throughout. Please call ahead if you have any specific needs.

Do you work with all hair types?

Yes — all hair types, textures, and lengths. Our team is trained in Type 1 through Type 4 hair, and we celebrate every texture that walks through our door.
