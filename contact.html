Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need a consultation before booking? +

For major color services (balayage, highlights, color correction) and texture treatments, we do recommend a brief consultation — either in person or via phone — so your artist can fully understand your hair history and goals. For cuts and grooming services, you can book directly without a prior consultation.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations (under 12 hours) or no-shows may incur a fee of up to 50% of the scheduled service. We understand life happens — please just reach out as early as you can.

What should I bring to my first appointment? +

Bring inspiration images — screenshots from Instagram, Pinterest boards, photos of styles you love. The more visual references the better. Also come with clean, product-free hair if possible (particularly for color services). Most importantly, bring an open mind and be ready to have a real conversation with your artist.

How long will my appointment take? +

Service times vary. A men's cut typically takes 45–60 minutes. A women's cut and blowout is usually 60–90 minutes. Color services can range from 90 minutes (single process) to 4+ hours (full balayage with cut). Color correction services may require multiple sessions. We'll always give you an accurate time estimate when you book.

Do you accommodate all hair types and textures? +

Absolutely. Our team is trained and experienced with all hair types — straight, wavy, curly, coily, fine, thick, chemically processed, natural, locs, and everything in between. Kassa specifically specializes in natural and textured hair. If you have specific concerns about your hair type, just mention it when booking and we'll match you with the right artist.

Can I request a specific stylist? +

Yes — and we encourage it. When booking, you can request any specific artist based on their specialty. If you're unsure who's right for your service, simply book without a preference and we'll match you with the best available artist for your needs. You can also send us a message through the form above if you'd like guidance.
